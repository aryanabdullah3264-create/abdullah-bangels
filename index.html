Sizing

Find Your Perfect Fit

Follow these simple steps to measure your wrist:

1

Take a measuring tape or a piece of string.

2

Wrap it around your wrist at the widest point (just below the wrist bone).

3

Make sure it's snug but not too tight.

4

Note the measurement in inches or centimeters.
